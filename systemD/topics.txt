--------------: scaling :----------------------

If the database becomes a bottleneck, I would first reduce load rather than scaling it immediately.
I’d introduce caching for read-heavy traffic, then optimize slow queries and add proper indexing. 
If the system is still under pressure, I’d introduce read replicas to scale reads. 
Only if write throughput becomes a bottleneck would I consider sharding or vertical scaling
